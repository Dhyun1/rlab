Principal component regression

\(y\) \(X\) Centerizing .

\[ y = X\beta + \epsilon \]

\(X\) \(n\times p\) , p , \(X\) PCA .

\(X\) Eigenvector \(r\) \(E_r\) , .

\[ X = Z E_r^T \hspace{10mm} Z= X E_r. \]

, \(r = p\) , .

\[ \begin{aligned} y &=X \beta + \epsilon \\ &= XE_p E_p^T \beta + \epsilon\\ &= Z \gamma + \epsilon \end{aligned} \]

\(\gamma = E_p^T \beta\) .

\(\beta\) LSE \(\hat{\beta}^{LSE} = E_p \hat{\gamma}^{LSE}\) . .

\[ \begin{aligned} \hat{\beta}^{LSE} &= E_p \hat{\gamma}^{LSE} \\ &= E_p (Z^TZ)^{-1}Z^Ty \\ &= E_p (L^{-2}) Z^Ty \\ &= E_p (L^{-2}) E_p^T X^T y \\ &= \{\sum_{k=1}^p\frac{1}{l_k}e_ke_k^T\}X^Ty \end{aligned} \]

\(L=diag(l_k^{-1/2})\), \(l_k\) \(X^TX\) eigen value.

, \(\hat{\beta}^{LSE}\) ,

\[ \begin{aligned} Var(\hat{\beta}^{LSE}) & = \sigma^2 E_p (Z^TZ)^{-1}Z^TZ (Z^TZ)^{-1}E_p^T \\ &= \sigma^2 \sum_{k=1}^p\frac{1}{l_k}e_ke_k^T \end{aligned} \] .

, , \(r < p\) , \(p\) \(r\) .

PCR \(\tilde{\beta}\) .

\[ \begin{aligned} \tilde{\beta} &= \{\sum_{k=1}^r\frac{1}{l_k}e_ke_k^T\}X^Ty \\ Var(\tilde{\beta}) &= \sigma^2 \sum_{k=1}^r\frac{1}{l_k}e_ke_k^T \end{aligned} \]
